Abstract
The aim of our project is to detect and track human face in a live video input using feature extraction and matching. The application of the project lies in several sectors. The motivation behind our project is to develop a method to recognize the state of a driver and alert him if the drowsiness level falls below a certain critical stage. The project has several approaches among which the most efficient will be chosen as per our application.
